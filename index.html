Our Work

Recent Projects

A selection of our recent work across London — from data centres and flagship retail to high-spec residential and access control.

Commercial Refurb · NW1
2 Triton Square, Regent's Place
Full electrical refurbishment of a high-spec commercial space at Regent's Place, NW1. Works included suspended ceiling lighting, pendant feature installations and full power distribution across the fit-out.
High-End Residential · HA6
Private Residence, Northwood
Full electrical installation for a large detached home in Northwood. Scope included recessed downlighting throughout, feature chandelier pendant, double-height hallway wiring and multi-room power distribution across two floors.
Education · HA8
North London Collegiate School Extension
Lighting and power installation for a new-build extension at North London Collegiate School, HA8. Works covered conduit runs, LED batten lighting, power and data trunking, and integration with the building's fire detection system.
High-Spec New Build · SE1
Bankside Yards, Blackfriars
High-specification residential apartment fit-out at Bankside Yards, SE1. Full electrical installation including flush-finish sockets, recessed downlights, integrated appliance circuits and first-fix wiring in a premium riverside development.
Advanced Fire Alarm · W3
Iron Mountain Data Centre, West London
Supply and installation of a VESDA aspirating smoke detection system at West London Consolidation 3, Iron Mountain. High-sensitivity air-sampling units installed to protect a critical data centre environment with zero tolerance for downtime.
Prestige Retail Fit-Out · W1
Hermès Flagship Store, Bond Street
Electrical installation for the Hermès new flagship store on Bond Street, W1. Recessed feature lighting, concealed wiring throughout luxury interior spaces, specialist lighting circuits and full first and second fix to an exacting retail specification.
Access Control · WD17
Ralph Lauren Distribution Centre, Watford
Supply, installation and commissioning of a Genetec / Mercury intelligent access control panel for multi-door management at Ralph Lauren's Watford distribution centre. Includes controller boards, power supplies and full door hardware integration.
